How to Activate My5 TV

Step 1: Get the Activation Code

  • Open the My5 TV app on your device.
  • Sign in or create an account.
  • Note the activation code displayed on the screen.

Step 2: Open the My5 TV Activation Page

  • Go to my5.tv/activate.
  • Enter the activation code.

Step 3: Start Streaming

  • Once activated, start streaming immediately!
